Caesar Salad

yummy yummy kinG salad

This salad is sure to make you fall in love with lettuce - - - because you wont even taste it.

Smothered in goodness, this caesar-salad recipe is sure to knock some sox off.

Ingredients

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • parm cheese
  • pepper and salt
  • Caesar dressing
  • croutons

Directions

  1. wash romaine lettuce, dry, and place into a bowle
  2. season with salt and pepeper
  3. sprinkle in parm cheese
  4. mix in salad dressing
  5. toss on croutons
  6. ENJOY!
