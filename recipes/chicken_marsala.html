Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • All purpose flour
  • garlic
  • mushrooms - sliced :)
  • oil
  • marsala wine or vino - however you prefer
  • chicken stock
  • cleaned water
  • seasonings
  • oil and a pan
  • love

Directions

  1. butterfly the fish... i mean chicken
  2. dredge chicken in flour/seasoning mixture
  3. heat up some oil with some garlic. i like frying the garlic whole - thats just me. do whatever you want
  4. plop the chimpkins in the oil - fry em
  5. flip the chimpkins after a couple mins
  6. remove cooked chicken - add in mushrooms and cook down until the water fully evaporated
  7. add in marsala wine and simmer
  8. add in chicken stock and simmer for a min
  9. add the cooked chickens back in
  10. let is all cook together for a lil bit
  11. serve it over potatoes, pasta, some corn - whatever you want
  12. enjoy it becuase this was made with love
